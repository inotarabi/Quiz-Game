Quiz Game

How To Play:
Choose Your difficulty and start your quiz. You will be presented by multiple choice questions. Select your answer by clicking on it.

Scoring:
Earn points for every question you answer, the faster you answer the more point you will recieve

Timer:
Each question has a time limit. Answer before the timer runs out to earn maximum points. In the end you will be able to log scores on a leaderboard and compete with each other
